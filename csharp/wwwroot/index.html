    Tips for better searches

    • Use everyday language - no scripting knowledge required.
    • Be specific about the information you want to retrieve.
    • Mention a time period when it matters (for example, "last 30 days").
    • Ask about users, groups, computers, or organizational units.
    • Results are not limited unless you specify (for example, "first 10" or "top 5").
    • Without a limit you will get every matching result.
    • If the results are incomplete or unexpected, refine the wording (for example, restate counts or filters) and submit again.
    • Large recursive queries (entire org structures) run in the background. You'll see live progress updates.
    • Use "summarize by" or "group by" to get aggregated summaries (e.g., "summarize by employee type").
